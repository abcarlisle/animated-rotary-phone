Core Object: BOM

Roles:
- Shipping/Receiving: Read BOM, check inventory
- Purchasing: Add PO, read vendors, BOM access
- Engineer: Add/change BOM, price entry
- Manufacturing: Combine parts, update inventory
- Paint: Update part status
- Sales: Allocate inventory, read cost
- Boss: Approvals on threshold

Forms are role-specific. BOMs drive data structure and UI.
