Project: Company Brain MVP - BOM-Centric System

Focus:
- Frontend: Remix (React-based)
- Backend: AWS (CDK, DynamoDB, S3, Lambda)
- Auth: Auth0

Guiding Principles:
- BOM is the core object; every role interacts with it.
- Role-based forms and access controls.
- Sleek, modern UI (Apple-style).
- Idiot-proof with backend validations.
- Customizable BOM imports per client.
- Fast, seamless UX.
